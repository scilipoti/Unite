# Jobs

Hunter - collects 1 food + 1 furs
Gatherer - collects 1 food + 1 wood
Farmer - collects 2 food
Woodcutter - collects 2 wood
Trapper - collects 2 furs
Miner - collects 2 ore

Woodsmith - converts 2 wood to 1 lumber
Tailor - converts 2 furs to 1 cloth
Blacksmith - converts 2 ore to 1 metal
